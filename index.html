About Fabian

Fabian is a UX researcher with a strong product sense and a fondness for qualitative and quantitative research. He has worked with various clients across different industries. His mission is to make time well spent for people by continuously improving products and services. As a sr. UX researcher, he applies his background in applied cognitive psychology and information studies to understand how people perceive, process, and interact with information and technology.

Fabian uses various research methods, such as interviews, surveys, usability tests, and analytics, to collect and analyze user data and feedback. He delivers clear and compelling presentations of insights that communicate user needs, pain points, and opportunities. He collaborates with designers, developers, and project managers to gather user insights and turns these insights into data-driven paths to product improvement.
